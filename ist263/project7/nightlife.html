Night Life in Barcelona


It is no secret the Spanish people like to have their fun! Barcelona is definitely the place to go to enjoy the social aspects of their culture and have a good time! The night life is definitely a big part of the culture there and something the country is known for, so it is only fitting to share the best places I found during my time!


The W Hotel Bar


The W hotel is located down the beach, right on the water. The shape of it is meant to resemble a sailboat and it lights up at night time, so you can’t miss it. We discovered the bar at the top of the hotel and it quickly became one of our favorite spots to go. It offers the most creative cocktails, a live DJ, amazing sushi, but best of all the view you get of the shoreline.

Paradiso



This place is another hidden gem of the city. Overpriced, but worth the visit once just to say you went! No matter the drink you get, there is so much production and imagination that goes into the presentation of it, it is truly one of a kind.
Just be prepared to wait in a bit of a line, due to how popular this bar is for both locals and abroad students!

Iberostar Rooftop


Some of the core memories I have from abroad are from my friends and I going to this hotel rooftop bar after class almost everyday. It was just a short walk accross Placa Catalunya and you are transported to a relaxing, beautiful bar top with a pool, lounge chairs, and seating areas.It’s great for appetizers, mimosas, and relaxing on a nice day with friends. On top of that, you not only get a great view of the main square, but also of the ocean.

Opium


My all time favorite night club in Barcelona. Located right on the beach, has the best music, and socializing aspect outside where you can mingle and dance. When you ask anyone their favorite, or most fun club to visit in Barcelona, most would agree that Opium takes the cake. You are always destined to have a great night here and enjoy the music.

Sutton


Sutton was one of our other favorite clubs to go to during the week, especially on Wednesday night's! It is another exclusive nightclub in Barcelona and they always have well known and famous DJ's performing, as well as VIP tables. You will always be sure to have a good time here, and it is located accross the street from a 24 hour pizza shop!
If you want to get a table at any club, I would reccommend getting one at Sutton because you get private access, more space, and a private lounge dancing area.

Shoko


Also located right on the waterfront, next to Opium. This club is the most fun on Monday nights and has a great outdoor social patio!

Razzmatazz


More of a techno, EDM music club in an old converted warehouse. It has 6 different levels and different themed rooms with various dj's weekly, so you will be sure to never get bored!
